Frequently Asked Questions

What types of data analytics projects do you handle? +
We specialize in Power BI dashboard development, Excel automation with VBA, advanced DAX modeling, data visualization, business intelligence solutions, and custom reporting systems. We work with businesses of all sizes across various industries.
How long does a typical project take to complete? +
Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple dashboards can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive business intelligence solutions may take 2-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
Do you provide training and ongoing support? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive training sessions for your team and provide ongoing support packages. This includes system maintenance, updates, troubleshooting, and additional feature development as your needs evolve.
What data sources can you work with? +
We work with virtually any data source including Excel files, SQL databases, cloud platforms (Azure, AWS), web APIs, SharePoint, Google Analytics, CRM systems, ERP systems, and more. Data integration is one of our core strengths.
How do you ensure data security and confidentiality? +
We follow strict data security protocols including signed NDAs, secure data transfer methods, encrypted storage, and compliance with industry standards. Your data privacy and security are our top priorities throughout every project.
